Dashboard Guide

Understanding your business pulse.

1. Key Metrics

Total MRR

"Monthly Recurring Revenue". The total estimated income from all active monthly subscriptions.

Active Subs

Number of paying customers currently subscribed.

Total Users

Everyone who has signed up, including free users.

2. Quick Actions

Shortcuts for common management tasks:

  • Services: Enable/Disable tools, configure webhooks.
  • Users: View account details, impersonate users for support.
  • Plans: Adjust pricing models and feature flags.
Manager's Pro Tip

The dashboard updates in real-time. If you see a dip in MRR, check "Subscriptions" to see if a payment failed.
