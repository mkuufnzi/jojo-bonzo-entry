# Floovioo Product Pillars

Floovioo is organized into four core functional pillars, each designed as a standalone branded product but powered by the same central engine.

## 1. Floovioo Transactional
**Focus**: Automated Branding & Document Delivery.
- **Value Proposition**: Return professionally branded document packages (Invoices, Receipts, Quotes) for every ERP transaction.
- **Core Workflow**: 
    - ERP Hook (Xero/Zoho/QB) -> Floovioo -> Branded PDF -> Email.
- **Key Features**:
    - "Canva-like" high-fidelity document layouts.
    - AI-powered upsells & cross-sells embedded in documents.
    - Smart support links and feedback collection.

## 2. Floovioo Sales
**Focus**: AI Proposal Generation & Sales Intelligence.
- **Value Proposition**: Close deals faster with interactive, AI-optimized branding proposals.
- **Core Workflow**: 
    - Lead/Opp Sync -> Content Personalization -> Interactive Branding Web-Proposal.
- **Key Features**:
    - AI-driven proposal drafting based on client niche.
    - Real-time interaction tracking.
    - Integrated e-signatures.

## 3. Floovioo Content
**Focus**: Marketing Asset Automation.
- **Value Proposition**: Maintain brand consistency across all marketing channels without manual effort.
- **Key Features**:
    - Social media asset generation from brand voice.
    - Automated blog post/newsletter branding.
    - Dynamic ad creative generation.

## 4. Floovioo Retention (Dunning & Support)
**Focus**: Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) Optimization.
- **Value Proposition**: Turn transactional moments into retention opportunities.
- **Key Features**:
    - Automated dunning sequences (friendly reminders) with brand voice.
    - Intelligent support document generation.
    - Feedback loop integration into transactional flows.

---

**Note**: All pillars utilize the **Design Engine** for layout and **n8n** for AI-orchestration, ensuring a unified brand experience across the entire customer journey.
